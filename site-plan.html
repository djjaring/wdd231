Pickleball Samurai — Individual Project Site Plan

Website planning document for a merch brand focused on Pickleball Samurai socks and jacket/outerwear.

Site Name

Brand / Identity

Site name: Pickleball Samurai

The name combines pickleball with the samurai theme to communicate discipline, performance, and bold athletic style.

Site Purpose

Scope

The Pickleball Samurai website introduces the brand, showcases socks and jacket merch, and provides sizing, care, and contact information.

  • Showcase sock collections
  • Showcase jacket merchandise
  • Explain materials and comfort features
  • Provide sizing and care guides
  • Contact form for bulk or team orders

Scenarios

Visitor Questions

  • What socks are best for long pickleball sessions?
  • Do you offer matching jackets for sock designs?
  • How do I choose the right size?
  • Can I order for my league or team?

Color Scheme

Brand Colors

Crimson – Buttons, CTAs, highlights
Gold – Accents, badges
Ink – Headings and text
Paper – Page background

Typography

Fonts

  • Oswald – Headings and section titles
  • Montserrat – Body text and UI elements
